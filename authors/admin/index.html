Samantha Robertson

First Year Ph.D. Student

Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences, UC Berkeley

I am a first-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences at UC Berkeley. I am broadly interested in applications of data science to human-centered problems, which has led me to research positions involving open-source software development and statistical analysis in the Stanford Brain Interfacing Lab, the Stanford Cardiovascular Biomechanics Computation Lab, and the Stanford Computational Policy Lab. I completed my B.S. in Mathematical and Computational Science at Stanford University in 2019. When I have free time you might find me at the beach (when it’s sunny), pro-craft-inating (when it’s not), and occasionally practising my Tidyverse skills on fun datasets.
